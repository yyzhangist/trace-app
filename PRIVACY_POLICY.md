# Trace Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** November 24, 2025

## Introduction

Trace ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use the Trace mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide
- **Email Address** (optional): Only if you sign up for the waitlist or create an account
- **Drawings**: Your drawings are stored locally on your device by default

### Automatically Collected Information
- **Usage Data**: How you interact with the app (anonymous analytics)
- **Device Information**: Device type, OS version, app version
- **Crash Reports**: Technical data to improve app stability

## How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:
- Provide and improve the Trace app
- Sync your drawings across devices (Premium feature only)
- Send important updates about the app (if you opted in)
- Analyze usage patterns to improve the experience
- Respond to support requests

## Data Storage

### Local Storage (Free Users)
- All drawings are stored locally on your device
- We do NOT have access to your drawings
- Data is not sent to our servers

### Cloud Storage (Premium Users)
- Drawings are encrypted and stored in Firebase/Supabase
- Only you can access your drawings
- We cannot view your drawing content

## Third-Party Services

We use the following services:
- **Firebase/Supabase**: Cloud storage and authentication
- **Apple App Store**: Payment processing (In-App Purchases)
- **Analytics**: Google Analytics or Mixpanel (anonymous data only)

## Data Sharing

We DO NOT:
- Sell your personal information
- Share your drawings with anyone
- Use your data for advertising

We MAY share data:
- If required by law
- To protect our rights or safety
- With your explicit consent

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- **Access** your data
- **Delete** your data (delete the app or contact us)
- **Export** your drawings
- **Opt-out** of analytics
- **Cancel** your subscription anytime

## Children's Privacy

Trace is suitable for users age 4+. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent.

## Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:
- Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS)
- Encrypted cloud storage
- Secure authentication
- Regular security audits

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy. We will notify you of any changes by:
- Posting the new policy in the app
- Sending an email (if you provided one)
- Displaying a notice on app launch
